BMW M3 E30 DTM

A Racing Legend

The BMW M3 E30 DTM is a legendary race car that was produced by BMW Motorsport between 1986 and 1992. It was developed specifically to compete in the German Touring Car Championship (DTM) and was based on the standard BMW E30 3 Series. However, the M3 E30 DTM was heavily modified for racing purposes and featured a powerful four-cylinder engine, a lightweight body, and advanced aerodynamics to provide maximum downforce and grip on the track. Under the hood, the BMW M3 E30 DTM was powered by a 2.3-liter S14 four-cylinder engine that produced around 300 horsepower, depending on the specific model and tuning. The engine was paired with a five-speed manual transmission and a limited-slip differential for maximum performance and traction on the track. The car's suspension system featured a MacPherson strut design with adjustable dampers, while the braking system included ventilated discs all around and four-piston calipers at the front. The car also had a unique aerodynamic package that included a front splitter, side skirts, and a large rear wing to provide maximum downforce and stability at high speeds. Inside the car, the driver was positioned in a Recaro racing seat and surrounded by a full roll cage for maximum safety. The car also had a stripped-down interior with all unnecessary components removed to save weight and improve performance.

Car That Redefined Touring Car Racing

The BMW M3 E30 DTM was incredibly successful on the track, winning multiple championships and becoming one of the most iconic race cars in history. Its combination of power, agility, and advanced technology made it a formidable competitor and set new standards for performance in touring car racing. The car's success helped to establish BMW as a leading manufacturer of high-performance sports cars and helped to cement the reputation of the M3 brand as one of the best in the world. The influence of the BMW M3 E30 DTM can still be seen today in modern racing cars, and it remains a highly sought-after collector's item for car enthusiasts around the world. Its legacy as a true racing icon is secure, and it will forever be remembered as one of the most important and influential race cars of all time.
